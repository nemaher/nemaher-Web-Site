Embassy Suites

This all-suite downtown Des Moines hotel has the privilege of being the closest hotel to the Iowa State Capitol campus. Our hotel is situated among the trendy eateries and shopping of the Historic East Village and the nightlife of the Court Avenue Entertainment District. We are across the street from the federal court house and just blocks from the state court house.

 

Holiday Inn

Since we're close to the Iowa Events Center, we're the best choice among downtown Des Moines, IA hotels for leisure travelers. Our vibrant city offers a wealth of things to do, and our dedicated staff can offer suggestions and directions to the historic Court Avenue District, East Village and the popular Pappajohn Sculpture Garden nearby in Western Gateway Park.

Des Moines Marriott

Capitalize on comfort and sophistication with a stay at Des Moines Marriott Downtown. One of the most convenient hotels in Des Moines, Iowa, this modernized retreat is ideally located in the heart of the Business District and connected to the Iowa Events Center and Wells Fargo Arena by a skywalk. Arrive in style, conduct business and mingle with friends in our cozy M.I. Great Room designed with three unique zones. Indulge in an innovative selection of American cuisine at Rock River Grill and Tavern and grab a cocktail or quick bite at City Center Lounge.
